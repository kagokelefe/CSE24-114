Our Story

Born from a lifelong passion for horology, Timeless began as a dream in the heart of Botswana. Our founder's journey started with a cherished timepiece passed down through generations, a constant reminder that true luxury isn't just about prestige, but about the stories we carry through time.

Since opening our doors in 2023, we've grown from a small boutique into Botswana's premier destination for luxury timepieces. But what truly sets us apart isn't just our curated collection of exceptional watches, it's our commitment to preserving and sharing the artistry of timekeeping with our community.

Every watch in our collection is carefully selected not just for its craftsmanship, but for its potential to become part of someone's personal legacy. We believe that a timepiece is more than an accessory, it's a companion in life's most precious moments, a silent witness to achievements, celebrations, and milestones yet to come.
